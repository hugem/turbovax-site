Frequently Asked Questions

Why did you build this tool?
This tool will not make it easier to book appointments, but hopes to at least help you reduce the effort required to find one.
Why couldn't I find the appointment that you tweeted?
Demand is incredibly high right now. Available appointments are almost always reserved within one minute.
How can I set myself up for success?
Login and pre-fill all required forms, so that you are ready to hit the "find availability" step at a moment's notice.
How long will this bot be up for?
Unclear. We want to keep this for as long as it will be helpful. We do not have any special access to propereity data, however, so our work could be undone anytime if authorities want to shut down this bot.
