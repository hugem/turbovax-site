Frequently Asked Questions

What can TurboVax do? What can't it do?
Simply put: TurboVax queries vaccine scheduling websites to find available appointment slots. It cannot book appointments on your behalf.
Why did you build TurboVax?
The existing vaccine scheduling ecosystem is a patchwork of sites that requires dozens of clicks just to look up availability. We hope that this bot will make for a more fairer (and less stressful) playing field for all.
Why couldn't I find the appointment that you tweeted?
Availability is limited and demand is high. At the very moment, there are probably hundreds (if not thousands) of New Yorkers looking for appointments on the very same websites as you are. In our experience, any available appointments are almost always reserved within one minute.
How can I set myself up for success?

Every second matters. Login and pre-fill all required forms, so that you are ready to hit the "find availability" step at a moment's notice.

For NYC H+H website: Fill out your data. To check availability, select I, [First Name] [Last Name] promise that... checkbox. If no availability, unselect and reselect the checkbox to quickly recheck availability.

For NYC Vaccine Hub website: Fill out your data until you are at Step 6. To check availability, select the relevant administration site and date. If no availability, click Previous to recheck availability at another site.

How long will this bot be up for?
Unclear. We want to keep this for as long as it will be helpful. We do not have any special access to proprietary data, however, so our work could be undone anytime if authorities want to shut down this bot.
